I am a researcher at the Computer Networks Group of the Institute of Informatics of the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), Brazil. I am Electrical Engineer (UFRGS - 2003) and I hold a M.Sc. (UFRGS - 2010) and Ph.D. (UFRGS - 2015) in Computer Science.

During my doctoral studies, I performed a one-year internship (ended in August 2012) at Cisco Systems (San Jose, USA). There I performed research activities as a member of the Network Operating Systems Technology Group (NOSTG) under the topic of Network Management.

I am a professor at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), where I teach courses on computer networks and security.

In this page you will find information about my current activities, the research projects I'm involved with, and recent publications.

Research and Projects

Broadly, I work with network and services management. My main topics of interest and research projects are listed below.

Topics of interest:
  • Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Technology applied to Network Management Environments
  • Network Measurements
  • Autonomic and Bio-inspired Network Management
  • Disruption-Tolerant Network (DTN) Management
Current Research Projects:
  • Participação em Grupos de Trabalho e Reuniões do IETF/IRTF do Comitê Gestor da Internet Brasil (CGI.br) - Utilização de Tecnologia Par-a-Par para Controle Autonômico Distribuído de Sondas de Monitoramento em Redes de Computadores
  • Programa CAPES/STIC-AmSud Cooperação em Brasil-França-América do Sul CAPES/CDEFI - Autonomic Knowledge Discovery for Security Vulnerability Prevention in Self-governing Systems
  • Programa CAPES/FCT - Um Ecossistema para Funções Virtualizadas de Rede
